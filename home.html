Welcome to ClassMate

ClassMate is an app designed to assist you during classes or meetings. With Classmate, you can stay organized and focused on your tasks without worrying about missing important information. Whether you're a student or a professional, Classmate can help you stay on top of your work and make the most out of your time.
ClassMate provides a variety of services to help you throughout your journey. ClassMate can automatically note down meeting minutes for you so you don't have to worry about ever missing important info again. ClassMate also provides a to-do list platform for those who tend to forget tasks to do.
